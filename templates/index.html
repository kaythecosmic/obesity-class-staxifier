Obesity Level Prediction System

Source Code

Obesity is a growing concern driven by sedentary lifestyles and poor dietary habits. Technology-based solutions like personalized fitness apps and smart kitchen technologies are emerging to address this issue. The lack of nutritional awareness and easy access to unhealthy food options further perpetuate this cycle. As a result, obesity rates continue to rise, accompanied by a surge in related health issues like diabetes and cardiovascular diseases. Addressing this multifaceted problem requires comprehensive strategies that promote regular physical activity, encourage healthier eating choices, and improve nutritional education. By addressing these root causes, we can effectively combat obesity and its associated health risks.

I developed an obesity level prediction system using a stacking approach with multiple classifiers. The aim is to use the ensemble learning method by combining diverse base classifiers into a meta-classifier capable of accurately predicting obesity levels across seven distinct categories. The model used, Staxifier was able to achieve and accuracy score of 0.9905 with a high precision and recall score of 0.991 and 0.9901 respectively
